About Centurio

Why

Have you ever wanted a way to get better at something? Learn to play an instrument, learn to code in a new language, get physically fitter? You probably have a busy life, and it can feel impossible to carve out the time to work on a new hobby or skill. As any athlete will tell you, the most time-efficient way to improve is to work within a structured training plan. Centurio provides that structure and tools for accountability.

What is Centurio?

Centurio is a web app to help guide you and keep you on track during your self-improvement journey. After you sign up for an account, you'll be able to pick a project. Each project is broken down into Days, and every Day has a task for you to complete. Every day, do what the project tells you and mark it as complete when you're done! When you sign up for a project, you'll automatically be added to a Cohort of other users who have signed up for that project at around the same time. Their progress will show in your feed, and yours will show up in theirs.

How do I use Centurio?

Start by signing up and taking a look at the project list. Right now, we have projects for:
  • Coding in Python
  • Intermediate Guitar
  • Journalling
  • Pushups
And we are working on adding more. If you have an idea for a project you would like to see, you can submit it on the Projects page.
